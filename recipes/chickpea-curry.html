Chickpea Curry

Ingredients:

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chickpea
  • Tomatoes
  • Potatoes
  • Coconut milk
  • Spices
  • Salt

Prep:

  1. Sweat onion and garlic and add spices to blossom.
  2. Add tomatoes and water and let simmer.
  3. Add potatoes and cooked chickpeas and let cook.
  4. Add coconut milk and bring to a boil so everything comes together.
