Coffee Ice Cream (base)

This is a recipe I used to make at my old job.

Unfurtunately I had no pics and got this one from the internet.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups sugar
  • 6 cups milk
  • 6 cups heavy cream
  • 560g coffee beans
  • 4 pinches of salt
  • 20 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp ground espresso

Prep:

  1. Heat milk, sugar, salt, 2 cups heavy cream and coffee beans. Bring to a boil, let steep, refrigerate.
  2. Strain coffee mixture, heat it up. Temper egg yolks. Cook until 180F.
  3. Strain. Add 4 cups heavy cream and ground espresso. Refrigerate before spining.
