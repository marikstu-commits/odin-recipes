Mini Cheesecake

This recipe yealds 30 mini cheesecake to be enjoyed at your next gathering!

I liked this one because it is chocolate cheesecake.

Ingredients:

  • 20oz cream cheese
  • 15oz yolks
  • 6.6oz eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4oz sour cream
  • 2.7oz heavy cream
  • 6oz sugar
  • 1.3oz butter
  • 0.6oz flour

Prep:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a mixer with paddle attachment.
  2. Blend everything and passa through a sieve.
  3. Press a crumble crust on the bottom of your molds.
  4. Bake with water bath @280F for 16 minutes.
