Ciabatta

This is a long fermentation bread recipe I make all often, it takes some time but it's super simple.

In the end you'll get 4 little breads for the week.

(Only 3 are shown because I ate the 4th.)

Ingredients:

  • 250g flour
  • 5g salt
  • 2g dry yeast
  • 215g water
  • 12g olive oil

Prep:

  1. In a bowl, mix together flour, salt and yeast.
  2. Add cold water and olive oil and mix until combined.
  3. Cover the bowl with a lid or plastic and refrigerate.
  4. After 30 minutes you'll make the first fold of a series of four folds.

    5. You can now leave it in the refrigerator overnight.

  5. Preheat the oven at 240C.
  6. Flour a surface and add your dough, divide it in 4.
  7. Put the dough on a floured sheetpan and let rise for 45 minutes.
  8. Bake it for 40 minutes to 1 hour until it get golden.
